Maze Extended

A complete re-implementation of my rather popular Scratch game, Maze. Experience the same puzzle, but with more sophisticated graphics (scrolling, yay!), slightly less boring music, and waaay more detailed instructions. Oh, and also, a 3x larger map! Hence the "extended" part. If you liked the first one but found it a bit easy, you'll enjoy Maze Extended. It's also the first publicly available preview of my new platformer engine, written in Processing 3 (a Java-based language and IDE). It's currently in Alpha, so expect some glitches, although nothing much worse than anything in the Scratch one. Download a copy for your platform and try it out! Then, tell me what you think.
